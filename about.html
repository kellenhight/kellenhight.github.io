About

My name is Kellen Hight (he/him), I'm an American student studying computer science and visual communication/graphic design. I'm deeply passionate about making useful technology easy to use for everyone, as well as digital freedoms as a vector for improving social cohesion and reinforcing real-world rights. Hobbies include playing guitar, sketching, listening to music, and finding new electronics projects to do.

For professional inquiries, please contact me at kellenhight@ksu.edu.
